Taco Seasoning

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 tsp oregano
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp (about 15 cranks) fresh black pepper

Directions

  1. Combine everything in a small bowl and stir until evenly mixed. Use immediately, or store in an air-tight container, away from heat and sunlight, until ready to use.
