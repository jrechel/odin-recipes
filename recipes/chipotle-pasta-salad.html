Chipotle Pasta Salad

Description

This recipe uses chili powder and chipotles in adobo to add a southwest flair that is sure to please

ingredients

  • 1lb fusilli (Rao's is a great choice)
  • 2-3 chipotles in adobo (add 1-2 tbsp sauce)
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1/3 cup red wine vinegar
  • 2/3 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2-3 tsp agave nectar
  • 1 tbsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp fresh ground black pepper
  • 1 poblano pepper
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1/2 onion, any kind
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes

Directions

  1. Boil pasta in salted water
  2. Put chipotles, adobo, garlic, vinegar, oil, agave, paprika, chili powder, and pepper in a blender and blend until smooth
  3. Roast poblano and red peppers on stove burner until well chared. Use wet paper towel to remove blackened outer layers. Roughly chop into 1/2 to 1 inch pieces
  4. Dice onion
  5. Halve cherry tomatoes
  6. Drain pasta and combine with sauce and vegetables, stirring to coat all ingredients evenly
